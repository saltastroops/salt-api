We are asking for your gender, race and PhD as the South African Astronomical Observatory is mandated to provide these statistics for SALT users who are a South African citizen or permanent resident. They will only be used for this purpose, and they will only be reported in aggregate form. They will not be shared with anyone else, including the Time Allocation Committees.

We are aware that when asking about gender or race, even the options in a form might be contentious. If you feel that our approach, which has been guided by reporting requirements, could be improved, please let us know.

Please contact salthelp@salt.ac.za with any questions and concerns you might have.
